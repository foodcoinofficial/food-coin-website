Our Transparent Funding Model

Our mission is powered by a clear, sustainable, and fully transparent system. It revolves around two core public wallets: "The Vault," which holds our main charity fund, and the "Funding Wallet," which holds the cash-equivalent funds ready to be spent on meals.

The Vault

Purpose: This is the project's main treasury, holding the $FC tokens that form our long-term charity fund.

How it's Used: We never spend directly from The Vault. Instead, its total USD value is monitored. When it hits a milestone on our public Funding Schedule, a small, pre-defined percentage is sold for USDC, which is then transferred to the Funding Wallet.

Address: YOUR_CHARITY_WALLET_ADDRESS_HERE

Funding Wallet

Purpose: This wallet holds the stablecoins (USDC) that are ready to be deployed for our mission. Think of it as our operational checking account.

How it's Filled: It receives funds from two sources: 1) The USDC converted from The Vault when a milestone is hit, and 2) periodic contributions from creator rewards (10% of every $10k earned).

Address: YOUR_USDC_WALLET_ADDRESS_HERE

Burn Wallet

Purpose: To create a deflationary aspect for the $FC token, which helps increase the value of The Vault's holdings over time.

How it's Used: This wallet collects all $FC tokens sent by the community for burning. Periodically, its entire balance is sent to a permanent, unusable "eater" address.
