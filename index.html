Funding Wallet

The Funding Wallet is being used to fund the meals. It receives $FC tokens sent from The Vault, which are then converted to $USDC and subsequently exchanged for Fiat to pay for the meals.

This is all performed following the mentioned schedule. So, for example, when the value of tokens in The Vault reaches $5k, 2% of the tokens worth $100 will be sent to the Funding Wallet to finance the first trench of meals.

Burn Wallet

This wallet's function is consistent throughout all phases. It collects $FC tokens sent by the community for burning to help increase the value of The Vault's holdings over time. Periodically, its balance is sent to a permanent, unusable address.

Instead of the community sending tokens directly to other burn addresses, this method of sending them to the official Burn Wallet will help all of us know the number of coins burned. From the Burn Wallet, I will personally send the $FC tokens to Solana burn addresses periodically.
